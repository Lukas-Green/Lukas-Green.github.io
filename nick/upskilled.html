Method

Four steps. No theatre.

We move at research pace when a problem is novel and engineering pace when it isn't. Most ML projects die in the gap between the two.

01 / Frame

Define the metric that matters.

Before the first model, we agree on what "working" looks like — and what it's worth.

02 / Prototype

Fastest defensible result.

A short, honest cycle that proves or kills the approach. We share what we find, including when we're wrong.

03 / Harden

Production engineering.

Eval, monitoring, CRS handling, tiled inference — the unglamorous parts that keep models alive.

04 / Hand off

Your team owns it.

Documentation, training, and a runway where you don't need us. We aim to be missed, not necessary.
