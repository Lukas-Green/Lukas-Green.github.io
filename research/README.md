# Digital Media Platform Research

Private competitive intelligence briefs for consulting workflow, client engagement, and contract scoping.

*Last updated: 2026-04-01*

## How to Use These Briefs

Each brief is structured as a consulting-ready intelligence document with 8 sections:
1. **Executive Summary** — Quick "so what" for client conversations
2. **Market Overview** — Size, growth, trends
3. **Competitive Landscape** — 15+ tools with structured comparison
4. **Pain Points & Gaps** — What clients struggle with
5. **Automation Opportunities** — Where your services fit
6. **Consulting Angles** — Service offerings, pricing, proposal hooks
7. **Client Conversation Starters** — Discovery questions, objection handlers
8. **Sources** — Links to data

## Integration with Consulting Workflow

- **Pre-call prep**: Read the Executive Summary + Client Conversation Starters before any prospect call
- **Proposals**: Pull from Competitive Landscape + Automation Opportunities to frame your value
- **Scoping**: Use Pain Points & Gaps to identify what the client actually needs vs. what they think they need
- **Pricing**: Reference Consulting Angles for engagement structures and rate justification
