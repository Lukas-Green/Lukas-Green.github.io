The Framework

10 dimensions, 50 points max

Every company is scored 1–5 across ten categories: alignment with current work, company viability, revenue potential at $175/hr, UX/design gaps, documentation gaps, consulting entry path, training opportunity, non-tech blind spots, accessibility (how easy to get in the door), and long-term upside.

  • Tier 1 (35–50) — Immediate revenue targets. High alignment, clear engagement path, strong budget.
  • Tier 2 (25–34) — Strong pipeline. Build relationships now, pursue when timing aligns.
  • Tier 3 (15–24) — Watch list. Monitor for changes in funding or accessibility.
