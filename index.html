About Me

I came up through graphic design — print shops, nonprofits, a UX bootcamp, a design degree at Portland State. I developed an eye for what works and what doesn't. But I was always more drawn to the system behind the design than the creative grey area. I think in structure. I solve in black and white.

When AI tools arrived and I realized I could build — not just design — everything clicked. Every project I've made started with a specific friction point: a team losing track of contacts, a contractor buried in invoice admin, a clinic bleeding revenue to no-shows. I find the pain point and I build the fix.

I'm a freelance consultant and I partner with teams who want to move faster and smarter with AI. I'm a nerd about systems. I love this work.
