What I Do
AI Consulting
I help teams cut through the noise to find where AI actually creates leverage. That means auditing workflows, identifying high-friction handoffs, and scoping the right tool for the job. Not the flashiest option. The one that still works six months after it ships.
Systems Automation
Recurring manual work is a design failure. Whether it is invoice generation, outreach follow-up, or data entry, I map the exact repetition and eliminate it. The automations I build are lightweight, maintainable, and invisible by design. Your team should forget they exist.
UX-Informed Design
Technical capability means nothing if adoption fails. I bring over two decades of design thinking into every build, which means interfaces that match how people actually work. The goal is zero training time. If someone needs a walkthrough to use it, it is not done yet.
Who I Am
About Me

I came up through graphic design: print shops, branding agencies, nonprofits. I spent over two decades developing an eye for what works across print, digital, and UX. I'm currently pursuing advanced studies at Portland State, where design and systems thinking keep colliding in useful ways.

When AI tools arrived and I realized I could build, not just design, everything clicked. I've since evolved into architecting automation-driven tools, internal platforms, and AI-augmented workflows. Every project starts with a specific friction point: a team losing track of contacts, a contractor buried in invoice admin, a clinic bleeding revenue to no-shows. I find the pain point and I build the fix.

I partner with mission-driven teams who need both technical fluency and design intelligence, especially where AI can reduce friction and build more accessible systems. Intentional design. Clean architecture. Smart automation. I love this work.
