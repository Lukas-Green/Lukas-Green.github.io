About

In a paragraph

I'm a data scientist working in applied machine learning, computer vision, and remote sensing. My current obsession is interpretable vision models — understanding why detectors decide the way they do, then using that understanding to build systems that ship.

Most of my client work runs through Upskilled Consulting, the practice I founded for engagements that genuinely need the research side and the engineering side under one roof. I've trained data teams at Fortune 500 companies, built production ML in precision agriculture, and contributed to the field through published research and conference presentations.
