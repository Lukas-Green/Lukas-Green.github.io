The Concept

Buy a template. Customize with AI. Ship.

SMBs in regulated verticals pay for tools that solve 10% of their problem. The other 90% stays trapped in spreadsheets, group texts, and workarounds that nobody has time to fix. The Industry Playbook Platform changes that equation.

Each vertical gets a pre-built SaaS architecture template — not a generic dashboard, but a solution designed for the specific pain points of that industry. When a customer buys a template, they go through an LLM-powered onboarding flow that asks the right questions: What's your clinic size? What EHR do you use? Where do change orders bottleneck? The AI customizes the template to their exact workflow, generating compliance-aware automations, documentation, and configuration.

The result: a production-ready system tailored to their business, built in hours instead of months.

The gap isn't a lack of tools. It's that every tool requires the customer to become the architect. We flip that.
How It Works

Three steps from purchase to production

  • Step 1 — Choose Your Vertical. Pick your industry: healthcare, construction, SMB office, legal, real estate, or restaurant. Each template is built from real market research and validated pain points.
  • Step 2 — AI Onboarding. Walk through a guided prompt flow. The LLM asks questions specific to your industry and business. It maps your answers to the template's configuration options and customizes everything: workflows, compliance checklists, documentation, and automation rules.
  • Step 3 — Deploy. Get a customized system ready to use. Local AI agents handle the repetitive work — filing, scheduling, billing optimization, field communication — while you focus on the work that matters.
