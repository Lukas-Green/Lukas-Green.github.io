The Approach

Know the market before the first call

Most consultants learn the client's tool stack during the discovery call. By then, you're already behind. The Digital Media Research Agent flips that — systematic competitive analysis across every major platform category, updated continuously, structured for immediate use in proposals, scoping conversations, and architecture decisions.

Each vertical brief covers 15+ tools with structured comparison: pricing tiers, target customers, API capabilities, integration ecosystems, strengths, weaknesses, and — most importantly — the automation gaps where your services create the most value. Pain points are mapped to specific consulting angles with conversation starters and objection handlers.

This isn't academic research. It's consulting infrastructure that compounds with every engagement.

The gap between a good consultant and a great one is preparation depth. Research agents make that depth systematic.
What Each Brief Covers

Eight sections, consulting-ready

  • Executive Summary — The "so what" in 3-4 sentences. Read this before any prospect call.
  • Market Overview — Size, growth rate, where the money is moving. Grounds your proposals in real numbers.
  • Competitive Landscape — 15+ tools compared on pricing, features, target customer, and market position. The matrix your clients wish they had.
  • Pain Points & Gaps — What clients actually struggle with and what's missing in the market. This is where your value lives.
  • Automation Opportunities — Specific places where AI and systems automation can eliminate manual work. Maps directly to your service offerings.
  • Consulting Angles — Service packages, pricing approaches, and proposal hooks. Turn research into revenue.
  • Client Conversation Starters — Discovery questions and objection handlers. Walk in prepared.
  • Sources — Every data point linked to its origin. Credibility on demand.
