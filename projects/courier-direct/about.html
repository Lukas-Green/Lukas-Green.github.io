About Courier Direct

A family business that answers the phone.

Barry and Sue Miller started Courier Direct in 2006 with one van, one phone line, and a promise: if you call, someone picks up. Almost two decades later, the phone is the same number — and someone still picks up.

Our story

Built in Tualatin. Still family-run.

Courier Direct opened in Tualatin, Oregon in 2006. Barry Miller came from the industry and knew the gap most customers were living with: late pickups, missed handoffs, and phones that rang through to voicemail at 4:57 PM on a Friday.

We started small — one van, one phone line, one promise. If someone calls, someone answers. Almost twenty years in, we're still family-run, still based in Tualatin, and still the first call for customers across the Portland and Vancouver metro area.

The fleet is bigger now. The service area is wider. The promise hasn't changed.

How we work

Four things we refuse to change.

  1. 1

    Someone always answers

    Nights, weekends, holidays. If you call Courier Direct, a real person picks up the phone. Every time.

  2. 2

    Drivers are vetted

    Every driver is bonded, insured, and carries photo ID. TSA certified for airport and Port of Portland work.

  3. 3

    No mystery charges

    Quoted before the pickup. Paperless proof of delivery. Invoices that match what we said on the phone.

  4. 4

    We know the routes

    Tualatin to Kelso, PDX to Eugene. Two decades of Portland metro knowledge beats a GPS every time.
