# FieldKit — Financial Projections

**Solo operator model | 36-month horizon | Three scenarios**

---

## Key Assumptions

| Assumption | Value | Basis |
|------------|-------|-------|
| Average setup fee (blended) | $500 | Mix of Standard ($350), Custom ($600), Advanced ($900) |
| Support retainer (avg) | $100/month | Mid-range of $75–$150 |
| Retainer attach rate | 35% of deployed clients | Conservative for service businesses |
| Consultant license fee (avg) | $1,000/year | Mid-range of $750–$1,500 |
| Digital product price | $75 | Mid-range of $67–$97 |
| Operating cost | ~$10/month | Domain + Calendly. GAS is free. |
| Tax rate (self-employed) | 28% effective | Federal + state (OR), SE tax included |
| Lukas's time value | $75/hour | Below market for this skillset — conservative |
| Hours to deploy one client | 3 hours average | Setup, config, testing, training call |
| Hours to maintain one retainer client | 1.5 hours/month | Config changes, support, check-ins |

---

## Revenue Streams Overview

```
Stream 1: Setup Fees         → One-time, effort-intensive, primary growth driver
Stream 2: Support Retainers  → Recurring, low-effort, compounds monthly
Stream 3: Consultant Licenses → Annual, relationship-based, high leverage
Stream 4: Digital Product    → Passive, volume-based, zero marginal cost
```

---

## Scenario Definitions

| Scenario | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Conservative** | Slow early adoption. 1 setup/month months 1–3, growing to 2–3/month by month 12. Digital product minimal. No consultant licenses until month 9. |
| **Moderate** | Steady traction. 2 setups/month from month 2. AppSumo launch month 6. 2–3 consultant licenses by month 9. |
| **Aggressive** | Strong early momentum. 3+ setups/month from month 3. AppSumo launch month 4. 5+ consultant licenses by month 8. |

## Sensitivity Analysis

### What changes if digital product sales are 10x higher?

AppSumo launches for tools in this category routinely sell 500–2,000 units. If a moderate scenario AppSumo launch moves 400 units instead of 40:

| Scenario | Base Digital (Y1) | 10x Digital (Y1) | Difference |
|----------|------------------|-----------------|------------|
| Moderate | ~$3,500 | ~$30,000 | +$26,500 |

Year 1 moderate revenue would reach ~$61,000 — comparable to the aggressive scenario baseline. This single variable (AppSumo traction) has more upside leverage than any other.

### What changes if digital product sales are 10x lower?

If digital product never gains traction:

| Scenario | With Digital (Y1) | Without Digital (Y1) | Impact |
|----------|------------------|---------------------|--------|
| Moderate | $34,675 | $32,000 | -$2,675 |

Minimal impact. Digital product is upside, not the foundation. The setup fee + retainer model is self-sustaining without it.

### What changes if retainer attach rate drops to 15%?

| Scenario | 35% Attach (Y1) | 15% Attach (Y1) | Difference |
|----------|----------------|----------------|------------|
| Moderate | $34,675 | $30,500 | -$4,175 |

Still healthy. The retainer is the highest-margin revenue but the business works without it.
