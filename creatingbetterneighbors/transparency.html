Art Inside Out in detail

How the flagship program works.

Created by Kirk Charlton during his time at Eastern Oregon Correctional Institution, Art Inside Out combines art and positive dialogue to inspire personal transformation.

The program

12 weeks · Art + positive dialogue

  • Self-directed art exercises Core
  • Facilitated group dialogue Core
  • Personal reflection & journaling Weekly
  • Life skills & positive thinking Integrated

Where it runs

4 active sites across Oregon

  • Eastern Oregon Correctional Institution Origin site
  • Oregon State Correctional Institution Active
  • Cascadia Health, Portland Community
  • Parity Wellness, Portland Recovery

Art Inside Out was designed to be replicable. If you run a correctional facility or recovery center, reach out about bringing the program to your site.

Who we are

Founded by people who lived it.

Better Neighbors was started by people who understand the system firsthand — because the best programs come from lived experience.

Kirk Charlton Founder

After 24 years in and out of prisons, Kirk created Art Inside Out at Eastern Oregon Correctional Institution. The program he built to help himself and his fellow inmates is now changing lives across Oregon.

Robin Davidson Founder & Director

Robin founded Better Neighbors to give Art Inside Out a permanent home and expand its reach into community settings, recovery centers, and additional correctional facilities.

Oregon Harbor of Hope Fiscal Sponsor

A Portland-based 501(c)(3) that walks alongside people impacted by incarceration, offering shelter, job training, and mental health services. They sponsor Better Neighbors as we grow.
