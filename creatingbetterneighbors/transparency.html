Quarterly updates

What changed, and why.

We log every material policy change, every corrected number, and every funder loss. Audit trail, not a highlight reel.

  1. Q4 2024 · Dec 15

    Block Grant cap raised to $8,000

    Board approved a 60% increase in the maximum Block Grant size after three cohorts of residents flagged under-scoping. Effective Q1 2025. Expected budget impact: +$180K, offset by slower grant cadence (target 120 / yr).

  2. Q3 2024 · Sep 30

    Corrected 2023 "neighbors served" number

    We reported 11,400 in the 2023 report. On review, 278 of those were duplicate intake records from the Navigator switchover. The correct 2023 number is 11,122. We notified funders and amended the report PDF.

  3. Q2 2024 · Jun 12

    Lost a foundation grant

    A $240K multi-year grant from a regional foundation was not renewed after a strategic refocus on their side (not a performance issue). We covered Q3–Q4 from reserves and won a replacement grant in October. Reserve dropped from 16 to 11 weeks, now recovered to 13.

  4. Q1 2024 · Mar 3

    Navigator stipends formalized

    Neighbor Navigators now receive a $600/quarter stipend (previously volunteer-only with expense reimbursement). Funded by a restricted gift. Retention expected to improve from 71% to 85%+; we'll report Q4 2024.
