Privacy policy.

Short version: we keep the minimum data we need to do our work, we never sell it, and you can ask us to delete yours at any time.

What we collect

When you donate, volunteer, subscribe to our newsletter, or apply for a Block Grant, we collect only the information needed to complete that action: name, email, address (for tax receipts and mailings), phone (when you opt in), and relevant program details.

We use cookies for basic analytics (page views, bounce rate) through a privacy-respecting tool. No third-party advertising trackers, no fingerprinting, no cross-site profiles.

How we use it

  • Process donations and send tax receipts
  • Match volunteers with programs
  • Send the monthly newsletter (only if you subscribe)
  • Report aggregate impact to funders (never individual data)
  • Comply with IRS reporting requirements as a 501(c)(3)

What we don't do

  • We don't sell donor lists. Ever. Full stop.
  • We don't share your information with other nonprofits.
  • We don't use your data to train AI models.
  • We don't share information with law enforcement without a warrant.

Your rights

You can request a copy of the information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Email privacy@creatingbetterneighbors.org and we'll respond within 10 business days.

If you're a donor and request deletion, we retain the minimum records required by IRS rules (typically seven years for contribution records). Everything else is removed.

Third-party services

We use Stripe to process donations, Mailchimp to send the newsletter, and Formspree for contact forms. Each of these has its own privacy policy. We've chosen services that are GDPR and CCPA compliant and that do not use our data for their own advertising.

Changes to this policy

When we update this policy materially, we'll post the change to our quarterly changelog and update the "last updated" date above. No silent changes.

Questions

Email privacy@creatingbetterneighbors.org or write to Creating Better Neighbors, 1842 Community Way, Portland, OR 97214.
