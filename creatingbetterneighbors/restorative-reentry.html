What we do

Programs that meet people where they are.

Inside a facility or out in the community, each program is a door back into a life. Our flagship has its own home.

Flagship program

A twelve-lesson art program created by Kirk Charlton during his years inside Eastern Oregon Correctional Institution. It turns making art into a way to change, and it now runs in facilities and in the community. Kirk's book puts the whole method in anyone's hands.

Why "restorative"

A person isn't thrown away for the worst thing they've done.

Restorative reentry means repair, not rescue. We don't fix people. We help them rebuild, balance, and belong. The strength comes from the mending.
