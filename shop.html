Questions
The short version
Why only six products?
Because 60 bad products is worse than 6 good ones. Every SKU is tested on a real garment before listing. Winners graduate to bulk runs with Portland screen-printers at tighter prices.
Why is the hoodie $29 and not $25?
A quality hooded sweatshirt costs more to make than a crewneck — it's that simple. Selling hoodies at $25 means either paper-thin blanks or losing money on every unit. Neither is acceptable.
How long does shipping take?
7–14 business days from order to doorstep in the US. Print-on-demand means nothing sits in a warehouse — it's made after you order. Trade-off: longer lead time, higher quality, zero waste.
What if it doesn't fit or arrives damaged?
Defects, misprints, and wrong items ship a free replacement, no questions. Buyer's remorse (changed your mind, ordered wrong size) is store credit only — we can't restock a one-off print.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes — US, Canada, UK, EU, and Australia at launch. Customs fees are buyer's responsibility. Other countries coming after Drop 01 performance data.
Will there be more drops?
Yes. New drop every 6–8 weeks. Some designs are limited runs (50 units, numbered). Email list gets first look. Get on it →
