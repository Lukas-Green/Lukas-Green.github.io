Who I Am
About
"I find the pain point and I build the fix."

I came up through graphic design: print shops, branding agencies, nonprofits. Over two decades developing an eye for what works across print, digital, and UX. Currently pursuing advanced studies at Portland State, where design and systems thinking keep colliding in useful ways.

When AI tools arrived and I realized I could build, not just design, everything clicked. I've since evolved into architecting automation-driven tools, internal platforms, and AI-augmented workflows. Every project starts with a specific friction point.

I partner with mission-driven teams who need both technical fluency and design intelligence — especially where AI can reduce friction and build more accessible systems.
